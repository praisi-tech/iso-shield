ISMS Maturity Levels

Level 1 Initial
Security practices are ad hoc and reactive with minimal documentation.

Level 2 Repeatable
Basic policies exist but enforcement is inconsistent.

Level 3 Defined
Formalized ISMS with documented policies and standard procedures.

Level 4 Managed
Security metrics are tracked and controls are measured for effectiveness.

Level 5 Optimized
Continuous improvement driven by automation, analytics, and threat intelligence.

Maturity Assessment Tip
Evaluate not only documentation but real operational evidence such as logs, dashboards, and response times.
