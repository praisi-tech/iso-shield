ISO 27001 to NIST Mapping
Access Control (ISO A.9) aligns with NIST AC family.
Logging and Monitoring align with NIST AU controls.

ISO 27001 to CIS Controls
Asset Management aligns with CIS Control 1.
Access Control aligns with CIS Control 6.
Logging aligns with CIS Control 8.

ISO 27001 vs SOC 2
ISO focuses on ISMS governance, while SOC 2 emphasizes trust service criteria such as security and availability.

Multi-Framework Strategy
Organizations often implement ISO 27001 as a foundation and map controls to other frameworks to reduce audit duplication.
